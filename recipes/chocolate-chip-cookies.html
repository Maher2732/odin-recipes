Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

You'll love these simple and easy to make chocolate chip cookies.

Cookies are delightful baked treats that come in an endless variety of flavors and textures, from crispy and crunchy to soft and chewy. Made with a simple base of flour, sugar, and butter, cookies can be customized with a plethora of add-ins like chocolate chips, nuts, dried fruit, and spices. Whether enjoyed with a glass of milk, a cup of tea, or as a quick snack on the go, cookies evoke a sense of warmth and nostalgia. They are a staple in many households, often baked for special occasions, holidays, or simply to share a moment of sweetness with loved ones.

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (14 oz)
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until light (about 1 minute).
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Roll 2-3 Tablespoons (depending on how large you like your cookies) of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
  9. Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
